# Event Cards (40 cards total)

Each card drawn at start of turn, resolved at end of turn.

---

## Patrician Decrees (10 cards)

**#1: Tax Day**
*"Lord Vetinari requires contributions to the city coffers."*
- All players pay 3 gold OR lose 1 influence token from any district

**#2: Breathing Tax**
*"A modest fee for the privilege of existing."*
- All players pay 1 gold per district they control

**#3: Unexpected Holiday**
*"The Patrician has declared today a public holiday. No work shall be done."*
- Skip all Action phases this round. Collect income only.

**#4: Guild Inspection**
*"Commander Vimes is investigating guild activities."*
- Each player rolls a die. On 1-2, lose 1 influence from their strongest district.

**#5: Regulatory Confusion**
*"New forms have been issued. Nobody understands them."*
- All contracts cost +1 resource to complete this round

**#6: Tuesday Rules**
*"Assassination is illegal on Tuesdays. It's Wednesday."*
- Assassins' Guild ability costs 2 gold less this round

**#7: Martial Law**
*"The Watch is on high alert."*
- The Watch patrols TWO districts this round

**#8: Diplomatic Immunity**
*"Foreign dignitaries are visiting. Behave."*
- No guild abilities can be used this round

**#9: Economic Stimulus**
*"The Patrician has released emergency funds."*
- All players gain 3 gold

**#10: Reorganization**
*"All guild territories are under review."*
- Each player must remove 1 influence token from the board

---

## Watch Activity (8 cards)

**#11: Vimes on Patrol**
*"Commander Vimes is personally investigating."*
- Watch raids TWO random districts. Caught players: pay 4 gold OR lose 2 influence.

**#12: The Shades Crackdown**
*"Even Vimes won't go in there at night. It's daytime."*
- Watch raids The Shades. All players there lose 1 influence.

**#13: Nobby and Colon**
*"They're mostly harmless."*
- Watch raids 1 random district. Caught players: pay 1 gold (they're easy to bribe).

**#14: Angua's Nose**
*"She can smell crime. And fear. Mostly fear."*
- Player with most influence tokens on board is investigated. Lose 1 influence.

**#15: Night Watch**
*"The city never sleeps. Neither does Vimes."*
- Watch raids The Docks and The Hub simultaneously.

**#16: Paperwork Day**
*"Vimes is stuck in meetings."*
- No Watch activity this round. All districts safe.

**#17: Carrot's Diplomacy**
*"Captain Carrot would like a word."*
- All players must give 1 favor to the player with the least gold (or ties split it).

**#18: Watch House Funding**
*"Donations to the Watch are... encouraged."*
- Each player may pay 3 gold to avoid Watch activity this round.

---

## Random Chaos (12 cards)

**#19: The Ankh Floods**
*"The river has remembered it's supposed to flow."*
- All influence in The Docks is washed away. Players there lose all tokens.

**#20: Dragon Sighting**
*"Probably just a really big chicken."*
- Random district (roll die): All influence there removed.

**#21: Festival Day**
*"The city celebrates... something."*
- All players gain 2 gold and 1 favor.

**#22: Fire at the University**
*"It's fine. The Librarian has it under control."*
- Unseen University district: No income this round.

**#23: Riot in The Shades**
*"Business as usual, really."*
- The Shades: All players there roll a die. 1-3: lose 1 influence. 4-6: gain 1 gold.

**#24: CMOT Dibbler's Deal**
*"Best sausages in the city! Probably!"*
- First player may pay 2 gold to gain 1 random contract card (draw blind).

**#25: The Luggage Arrives**
*"It's very loyal. And very hungry."*
- Place Luggage token in a random district. See Luggage rules.

**#26: Fog**
*"You can't see your hand in front of your face."*
- No Watch activity this round. All guild abilities cost -1 gold.

**#27: Beggar's Opera**
*"A cultural event of questionable quality."*
- Beggars' Guild player gains 5 gold. Others gain nothing.

**#28: Merchant Protest**
*"Unfair trade practices! Probably by them!"*
- Merchants' Guild player cannot trade this round. Others may trade freely.

**#29: Street Vendor Boom**
*"Capitalism finds a way."*
- All players controlling The Hub gain +2 gold this round.

**#30: Unseen University Mishap**
*"Just a small magical accident."*
- All players roll a die. Even: gain 2 gold. Odd: lose 1 gold.

---

## Opportunities (10 cards)

**#31: Black Market**
*"No questions asked."*
- All players may buy 1 favor for 2 gold (normally costs 3).

**#32: Guild Meeting**
*"Mandatory attendance."*
- All players draw 1 contract card.

**#33: Generous Patron**
*"An anonymous benefactor."*
- Player with least gold gains 5 gold.

**#34: Insider Information**
*"Someone knows something."*
- First player may look at top 3 Event cards, rearrange them.

**#35: Lucky Day**
*"The gods smile. Briefly."*
- All players gain 1 favor.

**#36: Trade Route Opens**
*"The Docks are bustling."*
- Players controlling The Docks gain +3 gold.

**#37: Political Favor**
*"You know someone who knows someone."*
- First player gains 2 favors.

**#38: Quiet Night**
*"Nothing bad happened. Suspicious."*
- No negative effects this round. All income doubled.

**#39: Guild Expansion**
*"Your influence grows."*
- All players may place 1 free influence token.

**#40: The Patrician Smiles**
*"You're not sure if that's good or bad."*
- All players gain 1 favor and 2 gold. (It's probably bad.)

**Card Back Design Note:**
