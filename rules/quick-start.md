# Ankh-Morpork: Guild Wars - Quick Start Rules

*Get playing in 10 minutes*

---

## Setup (2 minutes)

1. **Choose Guilds:** Each player picks a guild (or deals randomly)
2. **Place Starting Influence:** Each player places 2 tokens in any districts
3. **Starting Resources:** Everyone gets 5 Gold, 1 Favor
4. **Deal Cards:** Each player draws 2 Contract cards
5. **Shuffle Decks:** Event deck and Contract deck ready
6. **Place Watch:** Watch marker starts at The Watch House

---

## Goal

**Win by achieving ONE of these:**
- Control 5+ districts (majority of your influence in each)
- Accumulate 50 Gold
- Complete 3 Major Contracts (⭐)

---

## Turn Order

Play proceeds clockwise. Each round has 4 phases:

### Phase 1: DAWN (Everyone)
- Collect income from districts you control
- Draw 1 Event card (first player)

### Phase 2: ACTION (Your Turn)
Take **3 actions** from this list:
- **Place Influence:** Pay 2 gold, place 1 token in a district
- **Move Agent:** Move your guild member between adjacent districts
- **Complete Contract:** Spend resources shown on card, gain reward
- **Bribe Official:** Pay 3 gold, gain 1 favor
- **Use Guild Ability:** Your special power (once per turn)
- **Trade:** Exchange resources with another player

### Phase 3: THE WATCH
- Roll 1 die to see where the Watch patrols
- Players caught in that district: pay 3 gold OR lose 1 influence

### Phase 4: EVENT RESOLUTION
- Flip the Event card, apply effects immediately

---

## Key Rules

### District Control
- You control a district if you have MORE influence there than any other player
- Ties = nobody controls it (no income)

### Income
- Count all districts you control
- Collect gold + favors shown on each district

### The Watch
Roll 1d6 each round:
- **1-2:** The Shades
- **3:** The Docks
- **4:** The Hub
- **5:** Random (roll again: 1-8 for district number)
- **6:** Vimes is busy (no raid)

Players in raided district: **Pay 3 gold OR lose 1 influence token**

### Resources
- **Gold:** Universal currency
- **Favors:** Political capital (harder to get)
- **Contracts:** Complete for big rewards

---

## Guild Abilities (Summary)

**Thieves' Guild:** Steal 1 influence from adjacent district (costs 2 gold)  
**Assassins' Guild:** Remove 1 enemy agent/token (costs 4 gold)  
**Seamstresses' Guild:** Gain +1 gold per district controlled (passive)  
**Beggars' Guild:** Never pay Watch fines (immune)  
**Merchants' Guild:** Trade resources at 2:1 instead of 1:1  

---

## Example Turn

**You are the Thieves' Guild player:**

1. **Dawn Phase:** You control The Shades and The Docks
   - Collect: 3+1 from Shades, 4 from Docks = **7 gold, 1 favor**
   - First player draws Event card

2. **Your Actions (choose 3):**
   - Action 1: Place influence in The Hub (pay 2 gold)
   - Action 2: Use Thieves' ability (steal 1 influence from The Shades to The Hub, pay 2 gold)
   - Action 3: Complete a contract (spend 1 favor, gain 4 gold)

3. **Watch Phase:** Roll die = 4 (The Hub raided)
   - You have influence in The Hub: pay 3 gold (or lose a token)

4. **Event:** "Festival Day" - Everyone gains 2 gold, 1 favor

---

## Winning the Game

**Check after each round:**
- Does anyone control 5+ districts? → They win
- Does anyone have 50+ gold? → They win
- Did anyone complete 3 Major Contracts (⭐)? → They win

If multiple conditions met simultaneously: Patrician chooses winner (highest favor count wins).

---

## Tips for First Game

- **Start safe:** Don't go all-in on The Shades early (high Watch risk)
- **Diversify:** Control multiple districts for steady income
- **Watch the Watch:** Track where raids happened, predict next target
- **Trade:** Don't hoard resources, deals can help everyone
- **Adapt:** Event cards will wreck your plans. Roll with it.

---

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I place multiple influence in one district per turn?**  
A: Yes, but each placement costs 1 action + 2 gold.

**Q: What happens if I can't pay a Watch fine?**  
A: You MUST lose 1 influence from the raided district instead.

**Q: Can I trade anything?**  
A: Yes! Gold, favors, contracts, future promises. Negotiate freely.

**Q: Do ties in district control mean anything?**  
A: No. Ties = nobody controls = no income from that district.

**Q: Can I steal/block/sabotage other players?**  
A: Only through guild abilities (Assassins, Thieves) or some Event/Contract cards.
