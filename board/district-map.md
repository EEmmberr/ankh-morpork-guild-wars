## Districts (8 total)

### 1. **The Shades** (High Risk, High Reward)
- **Influence Slots:** 4
- **Income:** 3 Gold + 1 Favor
- **Danger Level:** ⚠️⚠️⚠️ (Watch raids on 1-2)
- **Special:** Required for several major contracts
- **Flavor:** "Where the sun don't shine. For good reason."

### 2. **The Hub** (Central, Contested)
- **Influence Slots:** 5
- **Income:** 2 Gold + 1 Favor
- **Danger Level:** ⚠️ (Watch raids on 1)
- **Special:** Connected to most districts
- **Flavor:** "The heart of the city. Possibly its spleen."

### 3. **Unseen University** (Magic Contracts)
- **Influence Slots:** 3
- **Income:** 1 Gold + 2 Favors
- **Danger Level:** ⚠️ (Watch raids on 1)
- **Special:** Unlocks special contracts
- **Flavor:** "Where magic happens. Literally. Often by accident."

### 4. **The Docks** (Trade)
- **Influence Slots:** 4
- **Income:** 4 Gold
- **Danger Level:** ⚠️⚠️ (Watch raids on 1-2)
- **Special:** Trade bonuses, merchant contracts
- **Flavor:** "Everything comes through here. Eventually."

### 5. **The Watch House** (Dangerous to Operate)
- **Influence Slots:** 2
- **Income:** 1 Gold + 1 Favor
- **Danger Level:** ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️ (Always watched!)
- **Special:** Controlling this reduces Watch raids elsewhere
- **Flavor:** "Right under Vimes' nose. Bold."

### 6. **Patrician's Palace** (Political)
- **Influence Slots:** 3
- **Income:** 1 Gold + 3 Favors
- **Danger Level:** ⚠️ (Watch raids on 1)
- **Special:** Required for Patrician victory path
- **Flavor:** "Where power lives. And watches. Always watches."

### 7. **Pseudopolis Yard**
- **Influence Slots:** 4
- **Income:** 2 Gold + 1 Favor
- **Danger Level:** ⚠️⚠️ (Watch raids on 1-2)
- **Special:** Balanced income
- **Flavor:** "A bit of everything. Mostly chaos."

### 8. **The Hippo** (Culture)
- **Influence Slots:** 3
- **Income:** 2 Gold + 1 Favor
- **Danger Level:** - (No Watch raids)
- **Special:** Safe haven, culture contracts
- **Flavor:** "Where the opera happens. Everyone pretends to enjoy it."

---

## Board Setup

1. Place board in center of table
2. Each player chooses a guild
3. Each player places 2 starting influence tokens in any districts
4. Place Watch marker at The Watch House
5. Shuffle Event and Contract decks
6. Deal 2 Contract cards to each player
7. Starting resources: 5 Gold, 1 Favor

---

## Visual Design Notes

**For Print Version:**
- Hand-drawn Discworld-style map (crooked streets, nonsensical architecture)
- Each district has a small illustration (Unseen University tower, Shades darkness, etc.)
- Influence slots shown as circles
- Income/danger icons clearly visible

**Color Scheme:**
- Earthy tones (browns, grays) for most districts
- The Shades: dark purple/black
- Unseen University: mystical blue/purple
- Patrician's Palace: regal red/gold
- The Docks: nautical blue

## Prototype Instructions

**Quick Print Version:**
1. Print this map on A3 paper
2. Draw circles for influence slots in each district
3. Use colored tokens/coins for influence (poker chips work)
4. Place tokens on circles when claiming influence
5. Use dice to track Watch movement

**Digital Version:**
- Create 8 district cards instead of a board
- Players place influence tokens on cards
- Easier to prototype/test
